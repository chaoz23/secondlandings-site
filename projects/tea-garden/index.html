The Project

After years spent directing things that lived on screens, I'm building something that answers only to soil, weather, and time: a tea and vegetable garden, by hand, in Renton.

It's slow on purpose — the kind of work that can't be rushed or optimized, which is exactly why it belongs in a second landing. This is the place I'll keep the through-line of it; the day-to-day lives on the feeds below.
