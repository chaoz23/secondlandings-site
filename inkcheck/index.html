01 What it does

Inkcheck walks through your story, tries different choices, and points out broken paths or places it could not reach. It never rewrites your work or judges the prose.

Bring the same .ink file you already edit—no conversion, repackaging, or special format needed.

02 Your story stays yours

Your story visits the checker only long enough to make the report, then it is deleted. We do not log your story or report. For unpublished or sensitive work, you can also run Inkcheck on your own computer.
