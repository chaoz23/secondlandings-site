Inkcheck

An independent, open-source second pair of eyes for stories made with ink, inkle’s language for interactive narrative.

Follow the branches. Find the snags.
Keep the story yours.

Check my .ink file → Run locally instead

01 What it does

Inkcheck walks through your story, tries different choices, and points out broken paths or places it could not reach. It never rewrites your work or judges the prose.

Bring the same .ink file you already edit—no conversion, repackaging, or special format needed.

02 Check your story

Use the hosted checker for a quick read, or run Inkcheck locally when your story should not leave your computer.

  • Uploaded only for this check
  • Deleted after the response
  • Not public
  • Not used to train or rewrite
  • Aggregate counts only

No-upload option

Run locally instead for unpublished, embargoed, jam-restricted, client, or otherwise sensitive work.

npx -y inkcheck path/to/main.ink
