What it does

Inkcheck follows a bounded set of paths through your story and flags compile errors, runtime failures, and content it could not reach. It never rewrites your story or judges the prose.

Use the same .ink file you already edit. There is no conversion, archive, manifest, or JSON bundle to make.

Temporary processing

Your source goes directly to the Inkcheck service, is processed in temporary memory-backed storage, and is deleted after the response. Story text and report contents are not logged. For unpublished or sensitive work, use the local tool.
